Privacy Policy for NetoSensei

Effective Date: May 4, 2026
Last Updated: May 4, 2026

NetoSensei is a network and WiFi diagnostic app published by Tosh ("we"). This policy explains what data the app handles, where it goes, and what it does not do.

1. Data the app collects on your device

NetoSensei stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Network scan history (WiFi networks you've connected to via the app, gateway IPs, latency measurements)
  • Speed test history
  • Diagnostic test results
  • Discovered devices on your local network
  • Stability event log
  • AI provider API keys you enter

This data is stored in your device's local storage and Keychain. It is never transmitted to our servers because we do not operate any servers.

2. Data the app sends to third parties

NetoSensei makes outbound network requests in three categories:

(a) Diagnostic probes. The app contacts public network services to measure connectivity: captive.apple.com, neverssl.com, example.com, cloudflare-dns.com, 1.1.1.1, 8.8.8.8. These connections do not transmit personal data — they are standard reachability and latency tests.

(b) Public IP geolocation. The app queries one or more of these services with your device's public IP to determine your visible location: api.ipify.org, ipinfo.io, ip-api.com, ipapi.co, ipwho.is. Each service receives your public IP address only.

(c) AI Assistant (only if you enable it). If you provide an API key for OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, Google Gemini, or Groq and use the AI Assistant feature, the app sends your typed message plus a snapshot of your current network diagnostic state (IP address, ISP name, geolocation, latency measurements, VPN status, and other connectivity metadata) directly from your device to the AI provider you selected. This data is sent to the provider you chose, not to us. Each AI provider has its own privacy policy governing how they handle the data they receive. We recommend reviewing the policy of any AI provider before you enable AI Assistant features.

3. Data we do not collect

  • We do not have a server. We never receive any data from your device.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or crash reporting services.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA).
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.
  • We do not require an account.

4. Permissions

NetoSensei requests:

  • Local Network access — required to scan for devices on your WiFi for diagnostic features
  • Location (When In Use) — required because Apple gates WiFi network name (SSID) access behind location permission

Both permissions are used only for the diagnostic features they enable. Neither is transmitted off your device.

5. Children

NetoSensei is not directed at children under 13. The app has no features intended for children. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your choices

  • You can delete all stored data by deleting the app.
  • You can revoke API keys at any time within the AI Assistant settings.
  • You can deny Local Network or Location permission in iOS Settings; affected features will be disabled.

7. Changes to this policy

We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page when this policy changes. Material changes will also be communicated in the app where reasonably possible.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: toshlabs.dev+netosensei@gmail.com
